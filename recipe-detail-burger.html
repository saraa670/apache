Classic Burger

Learn to make a juicy, flavorful burger.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground Beef
  • 4 Burger Buns
  • 4 Slices of Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 1 Tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup and Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 equal-sized patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill or pan to medium-high and cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side, adding a slice of cheese in the last minute of cooking.
  3. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  4. Assemble the burger: place the patty on the bottom bun, followed by onion, tomato, lettuce, ketchup, and mustard.
  5. Top with the other half of the bun and enjoy!
