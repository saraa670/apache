Vegetable Stir Fry

A healthy and colorful stir fry recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Broccoli
  • 1 Cup Carrots, sliced
  • 1 Cup Bell Peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 Cup Snap Peas
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the sesame oil and olive oil in a pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add all the vegetables to the pan and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, until tender but still crisp.
  4. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well and cook for another minute.
  5. Serve the stir fry with rice or noodles!
