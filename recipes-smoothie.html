Berry Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy smoothie made with berries and yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Blueberries
  • 1/2 Cup Strawberries
  • 1/2 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 Cup Orange Juice
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • Ice Cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place all the ingredients (berries, yogurt, orange juice, and honey) into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add ice cubes for extra chill if desired.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
