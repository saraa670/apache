Spaghetti Carbonara

A quick and easy Italian pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Olive Oil for cooking

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat some olive oil and cook the pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  3. Whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese in a bowl. Add some black pepper for seasoning.
  4. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain and toss it in the pancetta and garlic mixture.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, stirring to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve with extra Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
